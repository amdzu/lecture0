    What is Lukulab?

    Lukulab is a reading intervention that helps struggling and dyslexic readers become masterful ones and empowers parents to get them there.

    • AI-tutoring

      Our reading clinic provides intensive, AI-powered tutoring sessions that children can complete with any adult instead of a private tutor, making personalized instruction accessible anywhere and affordable to all.

    • Adaptive Learning System

      By continually tailoring lessons to the specific needs and talents of each child, our adaptive learning system gives children the skills they need to read for life.

    Our Approach

    • Comprehensive

      Lukulab’s tutoring sessions teach children to pull written and spoken language apart into its smallest parts, put them back together, and make sense of words, sentences, and whole stories. Children learn to guess less at new words, read smoothly, and find meaning in what they read.
